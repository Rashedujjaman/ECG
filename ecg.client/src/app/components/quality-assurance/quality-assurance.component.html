Quality Assurance

Quality lies at the heart of our sustainable retreading ecosystem. Everything we do is based on delivering the highest standards to customers. Testament of our commitment is our ISO9001:2015 quality management systems (QMS) certification by the Standards and Industrial Research Institute of Malaysia (SIRIM), which serves as the base for our quality assurance and total quality management (TQM) initiatives.

In continuing our milestones towards sustainable journey of retreading ecosystem, we are pround and honoured being awarded ISO 14001:2015 to be a part of the environment chain by complying the series of environmental management standards. We're driven by the efforts of systematising and improving our operation standards that aligned with our retreading ideology, practices and working culture.

By earning our basic certification marks, Eversafe's products are shown to be consistently manufactured with the standards of MS certified by SIRIM that boost customers' confidence and reliability.

Through our initiatives of 'Safe & Save' retreading concept, our offerings are more than just product innovation that are safe and reliable from customer perspective, but also save for the well-balanced environment in long term sustainable future tomorrow.
