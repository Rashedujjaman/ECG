Solid tyres branded "COMFORT" and "SMART" are produced at COMFORT GREEN TYRE plant in Malaysia. We employ the most sophisticated and technologically advanced machineries to produce solid tyres with radial matrices in six segments.

For solid tyres, COMFORT GREEN TYRE uses own compounds made with the best chemicals supplied by the well-recognized worldwide manufacturers. In such a way, through physical-mechanical tests, we can assure a constant quality over time, which places COMFORT GREEN TYRE solid tyres at the top of the market.

Key Features

  • COMFORT: COMFORT solid tyres have a tread pattern designed for any type of forklift, usage, floor and even for high load applications. They are available in two versions, the "TRADITIONAL" one in black colour and the "NO MARKING" one in light colour. The tread compound can also be in anti-oil, antistatic and anti-cut versions. COMFORT solid tyres can be produced in "DUAL-COMPOUND" version for static or very slow speed applications.
  • SMART: SMART solid tyres have been specifically designed to be used on electric forklifts, as they assure high smoothness thanks to their innovative "full profile" tread pattern smoothness that reduce the energy consumption of batteries for a longer charge of the forklift. By this brand of solid tyres, Comfort Green Tyre also introduced a new rubber compound for tread, having higher mechanical features. They are available in two versions, the "TRADITIONAL" one in black colour and the "NO MARKING" one in light colour. The tread compound is also available in anti-oil, antistatic and anti-cut versions.
Note: The model for quick coupling rims called "FLASH" is available for both types of solid tyres "COMFORT" and "SMART".

"COMFORT" AND "SMART" SOLID TYRES are produced in compliance with the following certifications:

  • UNI EN ISO 9001:2000 certified system
  • E.T.R.T.O. Specifications
  • ISO 2883 for antistatic versions
  • UNI ISO 1074 for vehicle steadiness
